Sunday, February 15th — The Wedding
Ceremony & Reception

The Wedding Celebration

Time Ceremony begins at 4:00 PM
Location Posada del Tepozteco
Paraíso 3, San Miguel, 62520 Tepoztlán, Mor., Mexico
Attire Vivid Elegance

Join us for an intimate ceremony in the garden, followed by a lively reception under the stars. Expect delicious food, free-flowing drinks, and plenty of dancing.

Tuxedos and gowns are welcome, as are suits and cocktail dresses. Our only ask: please do not wear black — embrace color and have fun with your outfit.

The ceremony takes place in a garden, so be mindful of your footwear. We're in the mountains, so we recommend bringing a shawl or wrap for when the sun sets.

  • Punctuality: Shuttles depart at the scheduled time to ensure everyone makes their flights. Please be at your pickup location 5 minutes early with all luggage.
